# Site Vision: ReLoop (Amazon HackON)

**Stitch Project ID:** 9839437392775019931

## Vision
ReLoop is a high-utility resale and logistics platform operating within the Amazon ecosystem. It bridges the gap between Amazon’s legendary operational efficiency and a modern, eco-conscious ethos by routing returned goods to local buyers or NGOs instead of shipping them back to warehouses.
